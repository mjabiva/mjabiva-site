About
the Work

Camera work across two of Red Bull's flagship dance properties — Red Bull BC One, the world's premier B-Boy/B-Girl competition, and Red Bull Dance Your Style, the freestyle street dance battle series.

MJ served as Camera Operator on the ground, documenting elite competitors and the raw energy of high-stakes live dance events — capturing movement at the intersection of sport and culture.
