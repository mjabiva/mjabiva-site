About
the Film

Sunset Roof is a music video for artist Malloy James — filmed on location in The Hamptons, directed and photographed by MJ Abiva.

Co-directed by Malloy James and MJ Abiva, the film captures the warmth and intimacy of a weekend with a summer fling, and what happens when it has to end.
