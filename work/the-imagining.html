About
the Film

The Imagining is a dance film by choreographer Amari Marshall, produced in collaboration with PowerHouse Arts Festival — a work that moves between imagination, memory, and the physical expression of the body in space.

MJ served as Director of Photography and Camera Operator on a multi-camera production, working alongside a full crew to document and interpret Marshall's choreographic vision for the screen.

Produced by Boat + Bridge and Boathouse Studio.
