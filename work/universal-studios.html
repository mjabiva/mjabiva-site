About
the Work

A campaign for UNIVRS — Universal Studios' street culture and art imprint — documenting the UNIVRSE 2023 collection; merging contemporary street art with some of entertainment's most iconic IPs.

MJ served as Assistant Camera and Photographer on the production, working at the intersection of brand, art, and pop culture storytelling.
