About
the Work

A campaign for UNIVRS — Universal Studios' street culture and art imprint — documenting the Buff Monster x Minions limited capsule collaboration, merging contemporary street art with one of entertainment's most iconic IPs.

MJ served as Assistant Camera and Photographer on the production, working at the intersection of brand, art, and pop culture storytelling.
