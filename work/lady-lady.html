About
the Film

Lady Lady is a dance film created for choreographer and dancer Edson Maldonado — a kinetic portrait of movement, femininity, and expression brought to life through intentional camera work and precise editing.

Shot and produced by MJ Abiva, the film showcases a movement-first approach to cinematography — letting the choreography drive the visual language rather than the other way around.
