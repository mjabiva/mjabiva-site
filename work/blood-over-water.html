About
the Film

Blood over Water is a short dance film written and directed by Gabriel DeJesus, photographed by MJ Abiva. A kinetic exploration of loyalty and tension you only express with your brother, soundtracked by Chance the Rapper.

MJ served as Director of Photography, shaping the visual language through a movement-first approach in camera work.
